What we offer

Inside Ethiopia Tours will always offer you an itinerary that is personally tailored to your needs at competitive prices. We are passionate about creating exciting and unique trips with our customers at the heart. Our specially crafted itineraries include historic routes, cultural tours, short holiday stays, business trips, one-day excursions, trekking and wildlife expeditions – according to your needs and budget. We are the Inside Experts ready to show you our fascinating and diverse homeland, with a deep respect for Ethiopia’s ethnic diversity, its culture and its landscapes.

Our mission

Inside Ethiopia Tours mission is to always offer our guests an itinerary that is personally
tailored to their needs at competitive prices by carefully choosing our suppliers and
providing with a true experience of Ethiopia and its culture while preserving both natural
and human resources of the local communities. We are passionate about creating exciting
and unique trips with the people and the planet at the heart.
